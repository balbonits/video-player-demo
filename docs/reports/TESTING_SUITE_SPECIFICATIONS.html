Modern E2E framework with superior cross-browser support and video testing capabilities.

Cypress

Alternative

Good developer experience but limited to Chromium-based browsers.

  • ‚úÖ Excellent time-travel debugging
  • ‚ùå Chromium-only (major limitation)
  • ‚ùå No built-in parallelization
  • ‚ùå Same-origin policy restrictions

Selenium WebDriver

Legacy

Mature ecosystem but slower performance and complex setup.

  • ‚úÖ Extensive third-party support
  • ‚ùå Slower due to WebDriver protocol overhead
  • ‚ùå Complex configuration requirements
  • ‚ùå Flaky tests without built-in wait mechanisms
